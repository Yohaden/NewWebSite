Hello, I'm Youenn Denis

28 years old, born and grew up in Brittany, France. Highly motivated creative motion designer and on set VFX around the world.

I'm working for two and half years with the SolidTrack technology. That gave me the opportunity to travel around the world and work on prestigious long feature films. Since the end of my studies as an audiovisual technician, I've worked as motion designer, mostly for TV branding, using a lot of 2D/3D softwares.
I also know the basics of programing and electronic as I worked as beta tester, soft/hardware specialist for SolidTrack.

I'd like to gather all my knoledge and use my experience to be in charge of the VFX process on movies. From the preparation to the delivery.

I have scheduled a training to be able to become a DIT this year.
